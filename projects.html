Amal Capital was founded and incorporated in 2017 as a subsidiary of Amal Bank of Somalia by renowned project management and finance professionals. The company’s key objective is to provide turnkey solutions for business planning and development, project management and project financing within Somalia and the East African Region.

The Company employees are highly-qualified and innovative individuals who have the necessary skills and years of experience to respond to the regions and Somalia’s, complex and challenging investment needs. Our consultants’ expertise spans various sectors such as agribusiness, real estate investment and energy. At Amal Capital, exceeding client satisfaction is the stock in trade.

We undertake feasibility studies on behalf of our clients to assess the economic viability of planned projects and assist in the preparation of business start-up plans and investor-ready Business Plans complete with market analysis, financial projections and KPIs.
