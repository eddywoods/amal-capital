About Amal Capital

Amal Capital was founded and incorporated in 2017 as a subsidiary of Amal Bank of Somalia by renowned project management and finance professionals. The company’s key objective is to provide turnkey solutions for business planning and development, project management and project financing within Somalia and the East African Region.

Real Estate

Amal Capital invests in care and continuous research to keep its clients up to speed on market trends and to ensure your real estate investment will always be a positive experience

Agribusiness

At Amal Capital our Agribusiness experts will always be on hand to take you through project conception, planning, framework, scheduling and delivery. We use tested tools, procedures and techniques to formulate projects that will deliver success for our clients.

Finance

Our team will source funding for your project from investors and our network of financial institutions which includes our own Bank. Leveraging on the existing networks of our Bank, our expertise in business development and experience of our experts, we will continue developing a working relationship with key Development Financial Institutions such as; IFC DFID, SIDA FAMO, OIKO among others to fund projects
